FAQs

Got questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about the GN128 Solutions AI-automation platform.

Who is the platform for?
Founders, startups, and teams who want to build apps, websites, or stores and automate their operations without needing a full in-house dev team.
What do I need to know about how this works?
We audit your current workflows, design the right automation systems, then build, deploy and maintain them. You get a connected platform without managing the complexity — we handle the setup, integrations and ongoing improvements.
Can this scale with my business?
Yes. Everything is built on secure, cloud-based infrastructure that scales with you — from a single workflow to enterprise-wide operations across multiple departments.
What's included when we start a project?
A discovery session, a clear automation roadmap, implementation of the agreed systems, and support & maintenance so everything keeps running smoothly as your needs evolve.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security, encryption and access controls, and follow best practices for compliance so your data assets stay protected at every layer.
